If you’ve been inside for days amid the pandemic,

you might be getting a little stir-crazy — or at the very least, feeling bored.

“Human beings are not real great about endless time at home.”

Color or doodle. Studies have shown that “structured coloring of a reasonably complex geometric pattern” can lead to a meditative state that helps reduce anxiety. Go for a walk. A study found that walking can make you happier and reduce feelings of boredom and dread. Sleep. Aim to get seven to eight hours of sleep. Do yoga. Studies have shown that yoga can boost your mood. Listen to or play music.
